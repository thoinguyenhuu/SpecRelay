# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the SpecRelay community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and open to good-faith feedback.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Respect security boundaries and do not disclose private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, threats, deliberate
disruption, or publication of private information without permission are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately through the repository's maintainer contact or GitHub
Security Advisory flow. Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions,
or ban participants who violate this code.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
